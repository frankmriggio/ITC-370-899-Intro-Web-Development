My Hobby: Attending Concerts

One of my favorite hobbies involves seeing live music performances, or concerts. I have been going to concerts with friends and family
since I was around 14 years old, and I have seen about ten different bands perform. Some of the bands I've seen perform include Blink 182,
Third Eye Blind, Kiss, Motley Crue, A Day to Remember, Wage War, and Slayer. My favorite band to see perform is A Day to Remember,
and I have seen them perform four times in my home town of Saint Louis. With the COVID-19 pandemic retrictions being lessened and concert
venues opening up once more, I am looking forward to seeing my favorite rap group $uicideboy$ perform in Saint Louis for the first time in their
careers on October 19th. While I enjoy almost all types of music, my favorite types of music to hear live include rock, alternative, metal, blues, and rap.
