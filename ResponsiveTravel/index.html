Enjoy the Sites in St. Louis

Welcome to St. Louis, the Gateway of the West


Lying in the heart of the United States midwest, St. Louis is a city bustling with adventures and good times. Whether your ideal vacation involves eating delicious food, attending sports games with die-hard fans, hitting up the town to enjoy the night life, getting up close with wildlife at the zoo, or even exploring historical locations, St. Louis has your vacation needs covered. You could even catch some fine arts performances at the St. Louis Municipal Opera Theatre, known simply as The Muny to the locals, or see some beautiful plant life at the Missorui Botanical Garden. Last, but certainly not least, you can witness an architetural marvel by visiting the Gateway Arch, the shining symbol of the city and what gives St. Louis its nickname as the gateway to the west.


St. Louis is a place that is full of adventure, with somethin to satisfy everyone's needs for a good time. One of the most popular places to visit includes the St. Louis Zoo, a zoo with over 11,000 animals, natural habitat exhibits, and no cost to enter. For those who love science, the St. Louis Science Center and the St. Louis Planetarium are important stops during your vacation, as you can explore the wonders of science and the universe. For those who are looking for some fine art to view, the St. Louis Art Museum is a wonderful place to visit, with over 30,000 pieces of art from various time frames and cultures, plus some unique exhibits like ancient Egyptian mummies! For the sports lvoers out there, St. Louis boasts two popular professional sports teams, teh St. Louis Cardinals baseball team and the St. Louis Blues hockey team, so make sure to catch a game while you're here!
