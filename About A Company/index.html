Welcome to The Pet Rescue Company!

Looking to find a furry best friend? Then you've come to the right place! Here at the Pet Rescue Company,
we make it a top priority to find a pet that's the perfect match for you! The animals that have been rescued by
us have been provided the highest level of love and care, ensuring that the pets we adopt are forever loved by
you, their future owners!


All of the animals that are taken into our rescue center are fully vaccinated and tested for contagious or
life-threatening diseases, like feline infectious peritonitis (FIP), to make sure that the animals are healthy and
safe to be around other pets in their future homes. If you are interested in adopting a pet from our company,
be sure to head to the "Meet the Animals" section of the website to see who is currently up for adoption.
Should you wish to meet one of our furry freinds, you can reach us by email, phone, or directly from our rescue center. Our contact information is provided down below.
