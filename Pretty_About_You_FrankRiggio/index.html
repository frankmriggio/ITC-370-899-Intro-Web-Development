This is a page about me, Frank Riggio

My name is Frank Riggio, and I am a senior here at Missouri State pursuing my IT-Infrastructure degree. As someone who was born into the digital age, one of my favorite hobbies and pastimes is playing video games.
I have been playing video games since I was around four years old, so it has been a passion of mine for pretty much my entire life. My favorite systems to play video games on include the Xbox One, the Nintendo Switch,
and the PC, though I am currently saving up for a new PC for a better gaming experience. My favorite video games tend to be competitive multiplayer shooters such as Call of Duty, Halo, and Battlefield.
However, currently my favorite video game to play is called Rocket League, which is essentially a fast paced, hyper-competitive soccer game in which the players control cars that can drive around and fly to hit the ball and score goals.
In summary, one of my favorite things to do in my free time is play video games, specifically those that are multiplayer-based with a large competitive community.
